ldap helper programs

These programs were contributed by the Nokia MeeGo folks.

The first 2 are perl and shell verisions of programs meant to be used as $GL_GET_MEMBERSHIPS_PGM (see this for more).

  • ldap-query-example.pl
  • ldap-query-example.sh

The third program is meant to be installed as an adc (admin-defined command, see here), and helps users change their LDAP passwords.

  • passwd

Enjoy!
